## Overview

Veins of Eridûn is a text-based adventure game that leverages AI to create dynamic, procedurally generated narratives. Every playthrough offers a unique story experience as the AI imagines and crafts the tale in real-time based on player choices.

## Features

- **AI-Driven Narrative** - Stories generated in real-time
- **Branching Paths** - Your choices matter and shape the world
- **Procedural Storytelling** - No two playthroughs are the same
- **Rich Fantasy Setting** - Explore the mysterious world of Eridûn

## The Experience

Dive into an ever-evolving adventure where the boundaries between player and storyteller blur. The AI serves as your game master, responding to your decisions with creative and immersive narrative content.
